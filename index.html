About Me

Who is Denwa, anyway? Glad you asked! I'm Dennis, but my friends call me Denwa.

As a life-long enthusiast, I hold myself to an extremely high standard. I value performance, scalability, standardization, and rapid development. I'm skilled in modern development practices, UI design, and UX. My versatility generates unique insights.

I've been shamelessly obsessed with technology since the age of 6. You see, destiny commanded that I defeat my brother in his favorite computer game, Command & Conquer. However, in order to do that, little 6-year-old Dennis would first need to build a second computer and a home network!

My aptitude led me to a rewarding career in IT support, starting in 2006. I've been privileged to work with the likes of Tesla and E & J Gallo Winery.

After many years as a hobbyist, I landed a hybrid development/support role at an awesome SaaS provider called Text2Drive in 2019. There, I rapidly grew into a deployment powerhouse. Taking ownership of my feature, I increased buy-in by a staggering 40% and cut time-to-delivery in half. My acumen created an enduring impact by solving human problems, not just technical ones.

Eager to take on more challenging tasks, I jumped headfirst into freelancing as a full-stack developer in 2021. My transitive knowledge and soft skills allowed me to grow quickly. I've already delivered several successful projects, each met with resounding satisfaction from users and clients alike.

Please feel free to review my recent work and my skills.

Work

TrapMonkie.io

This web app allows users to purchase NFTs (Non-Fungible Tokens) on the Ethereum blockchain, hosted on Cloudflare Pages JAMstack. Authentication and Authorization are generated from injected Ethereum wallet context. I used merkletreejs to generate Merkle proofs, making privileged transactions more efficient.

EthereumHomies.com

The predecessor to my TrapMonkie app, this web app also allows users to purchase NFTs (Non-Fungible Tokens) on the Ethereum blockchain, hosted on Netlify JAMstack. I built it using an open-source React/Redux template by HashLips. I added a 3D model viewer using react-three-fiber. Authentication and Authorization are generated from injected Ethereum wallet context.
