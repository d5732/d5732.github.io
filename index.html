About Me

I'm a Full-Stack Developer with a passion for secure, scalable releases. Feel free to review my recent work and certifications.

I'm skilled in React, JavaScript, Node.js, PostGreSQL, Express and SCSS. I also dabble in UI/UX and Graphic Design.

After switching from a career in tech/web/telecom support, I've self-taught and honed my skills as a full-stack developer. In 2019, I obtained a hybrid front-end/support role at a SaaS where I designed small front-end components and learned about online payments, web, and telecom infrastructure. I like to focus on responsive design, modern UX, and accessibility to create high-quality web applications.

When I'm not bathing in the blue tint of my monitor, I enjoy DIY projects, gardening, and playing guitar.

Work

TrapMonkie.io

This web app allows users to purchase NFTs (Non-Fungible Tokens) on the Ethereum blockchain, hosted on Cloudflare Pages JAMstack. Authentication and Authorization are generated from injected Ethereum wallet context. I used merkletreejs to generate Merkle proofs, making privileged transactions more efficient.

EthereumHomies.com

The predecessor to my TrapMonkie app, this web app also allows users to purchase NFTs (Non-Fungible Tokens) on the Ethereum blockchain, hosted on Netlify JAMstack. I built it using an open-source React/Redux template by HashLips. I added a 3D model viewer using react-three-fiber. Authentication and Authorization are generated from injected Ethereum wallet context.
